Steamed Prawns

Description

Easy, spicy, steamed shrimp with Old Bay seasoning. Great for a super-quick

dinner with any salad.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup of water
  • 0.5 cup of white vinegar
  • 2 tbsp of seafood seasoning
  • 1 pound of large prawns

Instructions

  1. Bring water, vinegar, and seafood seasoning to a boil in a saucepan over high heat. Add shrimp and stir.
  2. Reduce heat to medium, cover, and steam, stirring once or twice, until bright orange in color, 3 to 5 minutes. Drain.
