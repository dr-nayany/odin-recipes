Fluffy Microwave Eggs

Description

Use your microwave to make light and fluffy scrambled eggs for a quick and

easy breakfast to start your day. Follow the technique in this recipe for perfect

results every time.

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 0.25 cup of milk
  • 0.125 teaspoon of salt

Instructions

  1. Crack eggs into a microwave safe bowl then add the salt and milk. Stir well.
  2. Microwave in 30 second interval, scraping and mixing between each, until ready.
  3. Serve warm on a toasted slice of bread and enjoy.
